**ETH is available on the following chains within the NEAR Intents / Defuse 1 Click ecosystem**

| Chain | How ETH appears there | Typical use case |
|-------|----------------------|-----------------|
| **NEAR** | Wrapped ETH (often shown simply as **ETH**) that lives on the NEAR blockchain via the official bridge. | Swap to any NEAR native token (e.g., NEAR, USDT, wBTC, etc.) without leaving the NEAR network. |
| **Aurora** | Aurora is NEAR s EVM compatible side chain, so ETH is supported as a native compatible asset (you can treat it like regular ETH on an EVM). | Move ETH into Aurora based DeFi protocols or bridge it back to NEAR wrapped ETH for further swaps. |
| **Ethereum (Mainnet)**   via the cross chain bridge | The same ETH you hold on Ethereum can be bridged into NEAR (becoming the wrapped ETH above). | If you already have ETH on Ethereum, you can bridge it to NEAR and then swap it to any of the other supported tokens. |

In short, **the only  chains  where you can directly work with ETH on our platform are NEAR and Aurora** (both part of the NEAR ecosystem). Any ETH you have on the Ethereum mainnet must first be bridged into NEAR, after which it appears as the wrapped ETH token listed above.
